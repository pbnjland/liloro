Laeral Silverhand (pronounced: /ˈ[l](https://forgottenrealms.fandom.com/wiki/L_as_in_%22law%22)[ɛər](https://forgottenrealms.fandom.com/wiki/Air_as_in_%22air%22)[ɔː](https://forgottenrealms.fandom.com/wiki/Aw_as_in_%22law%22)[l](https://forgottenrealms.fandom.com/wiki/L_as_in_%22law%22)/ LAIR-awl[[13]](https://forgottenrealms.fandom.com/wiki/Laeral_Silverhand#cite_note-WDH5e-p4-13)), born Anamanué Silverhand and formerly known as the Witch-Queen of the North[[3]](https://forgottenrealms.fandom.com/wiki/Laeral_Silverhand#cite_note-WDH5e-p207-3) and later the Lady Mage of Waterdeep, was one of the [Seven Sisters](https://forgottenrealms.fandom.com/wiki/Seven_Sisters), a [Chosen of Mystra](https://forgottenrealms.fandom.com/wiki/Chosen_of_Mystra),[[12]](https://forgottenrealms.fandom.com/wiki/Laeral_Silverhand#cite_note-FRCS3e-280-12) and the [Open Lord](https://forgottenrealms.fandom.com/wiki/Open_Lord) of [Waterdeep](https://forgottenrealms.fandom.com/wiki/Waterdeep) during the late 15th century [DR](https://forgottenrealms.fandom.com/wiki/DR).[[3]](https://forgottenrealms.fandom.com/wiki/Laeral_Silverhand#cite_note-WDH5e-p207-3) Together with her partner [Khelben "Blackstaff" Arunsun](https://forgottenrealms.fandom.com/wiki/Khelben_%22Blackstaff%22_Arunsun), they led the [Moonstars](https://forgottenrealms.fandom.com/wiki/Moonstars), a dissident faction of the [Harpers](https://forgottenrealms.fandom.com/wiki/Harpers), which sought to guide the cities and countries of the [North](https://forgottenrealms.fandom.com/wiki/North) toward a shared destiny.[[12]](https://forgottenrealms.fandom.com/wiki/Laeral_Silverhand#cite_note-FRCS3e-280-12)
